Monapo Font
===========

Source: Debian fonts-monapo package (fonts-monapo_20170722-3_all.deb)
URL: https://packages.debian.org/stable/fonts-monapo

Monapo is a Japanese proportional Gothic font derived from IPA Font,
with glyph width adjustments for 2ch/5ch ASCII Art (AA) compatibility.
